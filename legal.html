Because bets are stakes on events, confirmed bets are generally final and non-refundable. This policy explains exceptional circumstances and the handling of deposits and withdrawals.

4. Bets & Voiding

  • Confirmed bets are non-refundable.
  • Bets may be voided and refunded only where:
    • There was a clear platform or acceptance error (e.g. duplicate acceptance or pricing error).
    • The event is cancelled and the relevant market rules require refunds.
    • Proven fraud or system compromise affecting the bet.
  • In case of a voided market, we will refund the stake to the user’s PakBet wallet.

5. Processing Times

Refund and withdrawal reviews are typically processed within 3–7 business days. Approved refunds to external payment methods may take up to 14 business days depending on the provider.

6. How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact support at support@pakbet.example with transaction details and reason.
  2. Provide ID and any requested documentation for verification.
  3. We will investigate and respond within the processing window above.

7. Fees & Deductions

Where refunds are issued, we will attempt to refund the full amount. If payment processor fees were incurred and non-recoverable, a fee deduction may apply; we will disclose any such deduction when we communicate our decision.
